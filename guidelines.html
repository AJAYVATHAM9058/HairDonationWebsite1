Guidelines For Acceptable Hair Donations


Cut

Zip & Mail




  • You can get the hair cut at any salon. Hair has to be washed,
    dried and tied into a plait with rubber bands at both the ends before cutting.

  • Minimum acceptable length for hair donation is a hair plait of 8 inches.
    Hair shorter than that is wasted as it cannot be used for making wigs.

  • Do not let the hair to be donated fall on ground as hair swept off the floor is unusable for making wigs.

  • Place the cut plait in a plastic bag with the name, email address and mobile number.

  • Please Email at urs@Hairdonation.org     your pre - post pictures of hair cut along with your thoughts about hair donation.
